Where to Put the Terms of Use
For a platform like DeckForge, the best placement is:
- Footer link on every page — “Terms of Use” or “Terms”.
- Dedicated /terms route — clean, easy to link, easy to update.
- Required acceptance during account creation — a checkbox linking to the Terms.
- Optional reminder when submitting a deck to the public marketplace — “By submitting, you agree to the Terms of Use.”
This keeps you covered legally and ensures users can always find the rules.
