🧠 What is Deck Foundry?

Deck Foundry is a customizable Discord bot platform for creating, collecting, and trading themed card decks. Whether you're into rockets, memes, fantasy, or fandoms — Deck Foundry lets you build your own universe.

🔗 Invite Deck Foundry to your server

Access Requirements:

  • Any Discord account can log in to create and manage card decks.
  • To assign a deck to a server, you must have Manage Server permission in that server.
